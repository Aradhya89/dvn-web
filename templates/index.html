About DVN

Data Vibe Network (DVN) is a technology-driven student club that brings together learners interested in AI/ML, Web Development, App Development, Data Science, Cybersecurity and automation. Our focus is on hands-on learning, projects and teamwork.

Our Mission

To create a practical learning culture where students build real projects, improve problem-solving and stay industry-ready.

Our Vision

To become the most impactful tech community on campus, known for consistency, collaboration and quality work.
